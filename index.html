HOW I CREATE MY DESIGNS?

01.

BRIEF

At the briefing stage, I collect as much information, instructions and your wishes, as possible, regarding the creation of the design.

02.

ANALYTICS

Based on brief. I research your product, compose a portrait of the target audience and conduct a competitive analysis.

03.

VISUAL CONCEPT

I start working on the design of one page (I add colors, photos / videos and UI elements) so that we decide on the general concept of the site. I send the finished design concept for evaluation. If necessary, we make adjustments and approve the concept.

04.

DESIGN AND ADAPTATIONS

I design all pages. At the end of the design, I present the finished pages, if necessary, I make changes. Also at this stage I make adaptive versions of the site.

05.

FINALIZATION

I am preparing the project for transfer, UI-kit. I hand over the project, completely ready for further development with a UI kit and other documentation.
